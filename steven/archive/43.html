Microsoft and DevExpress announce free CodeRush Xpress for C#

This was announced some time ago at the PDC2008, but I just found out after watching the PDC session on Tips And Tracks For The Visal C# IDE.

CodeRush is a extension for the Visual Studio IDE that improves productivity while coding. It's comparable to JetBrains' ReSharper, or as DevExpress puts it:
CodeRush Xpress is a comprehensive IDE Productivity toolset specifically designed to help you simplify and shape complex code – making it easier to read and less costly to maintain.

There's also a free ASP.NET version.

Have fun.
