CuttingEdge.Conditions in MSDN Magazine Toolbox column

In the November 2009 issue of his MSDN Magazine Toolbox column, Scott Mitchell writes about CuttingEdge.Conditions.

Of course I think this is great news. MSDN Magazine has a large audience and I hope this increases the interest in my library, because this can lead to an larger user community and an increase of the amount of feedback on Conditions which will further increase the quality of it.

While I am very pleased with this advertisement, I regret the fact that Scott uses the old extension method syntax in his Toolbox column. A syntax where the Requires() method is written as extension method (such as 'a.Requires()') isn't supported anymore (for reasons I explained here).

While promoting his Toolbox column on his weblog on the other hand, Scott uses the supported syntax. That gives me the impression that he wrote the text for his Toolbox column some time ago.

Nevertheless I’m very pleased with Scott’s review!
